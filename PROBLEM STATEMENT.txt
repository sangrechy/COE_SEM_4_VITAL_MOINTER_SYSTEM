PS2: Vital Monitoring with edge ai
Description:
A wearable multi-sensor patch that continuously monitors vitals (ECG, SpO₂, hydration, micro-movement etc.,) and sends anomalies to clinicians via edge-AI. Hospitals, home-care providers, and chronic patients benefit.
USP is ultra-thin batteryless design.
Scales into hospital platforms and telehealth. SaaS alerts + device sales. Reduces clinic visits, improving energy/resource use.
